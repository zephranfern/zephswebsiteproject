3. A description of accessibility of websites for disabled people.

When it comes to making your website more accessible to disabled people, you should make this one of your top priorities because if your website can’t cater to everyone regardless of disability not only are you loosing potential customers but you could be penalised by law. Here are a few ways to make your website more accessible to disabled people. For starts you need to make sure that your website can function without the use of a mouse. You could add alt text and add resizable text that doesn’t break your website for those with visual impairment. For more tips visit https://www.dreamhost.com/blog/make-your-website-accessible/.
