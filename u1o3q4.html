4. How would you go about testing digital solutions?

When testing digital solutions there are several things you need to consider, like who is the target audience, the functions of different devices, multiple session login, etc. So its important to test not only on the software itself but across multiple devices and age groups. You need to make sure that the site can function no matter what you do to the window size. You need to make sure that your target audience can use the site as intended. You may also what to try accessing your sight from different web browsers and see if you get the desired results. For more tips on digital testing visit https://www.deeperthanblue.co.uk/a-guide-to-device-testing-for-digital-solutions/.
