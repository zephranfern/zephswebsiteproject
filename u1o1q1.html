1. What is the purpose of IT in modern business and why do we need it?

IT has become essential to modern business as most data is stored online and communication is faster and more efficient. It can speed up the time it takes businesses to release products to the market as well as see how sales are doing in almost real time. IT will significantly increase production speed, advertising and, and even though the initial cost to set them up can be high the long term results will greatly increase cost efficiency. IT is also highly effective when it comes to global communication and can make it easier to expand and grow not just locally but internationally as well.
