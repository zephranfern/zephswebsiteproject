Unit 2 Outcome 1

1. A description of the content and layout of each page.

When you first open the sight you will arrive at my About Me page. There is an image of the Code Nation logo at the top left that will take you to the Code Nation website. At the top right there are four pictures with text underneath them that will take you to the other areas of the site. On each of the information pages there is a small Code Nation logo at the top right the navigation bar at the bottom right. The information is below the Code Nation logo and below all the text there are two arrows one that takes you to the next section and one to take you back. If you're at the beginning or the end of the section the arrow will be blue indeed of green and it will just take you to the same page you were on currently.
