2. A description of ways to improve productivity and efficiency.

Working in a team has proven to be much more productive rather than working solo as working solo means that one person has to for fill all areas of the brief themselves. Another way to improve efficiency is to upgrade the machines in the workplace. If the machines run to slow and crash often it can cause companies to lose out on money. There are also other ways to improve productivity and efficiency in the workplace environment such as taking breaks from work and quit multitasking. For more tips on improving work productivity and efficiency visit https://www.inc.com/john-rampton/15-ways-to-increase-productivity-at-work.html.
